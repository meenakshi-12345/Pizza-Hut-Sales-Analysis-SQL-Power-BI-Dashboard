# 🍕 Pizza Hut Sales Analysis | SQL & Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end sales analysis of Pizza Hut using **SQL** for data exploration and **Power BI** for interactive dashboard development. The objective is to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and product popularity to generate actionable business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Pizza Hut wants to understand:

- Which pizzas generate the highest and lowest revenue?
- Which pizza categories and sizes are most preferred by customers?
- How do customer orders vary over time?
- Which products consistently perform well across multiple metrics?
- What business opportunities can improve sales and customer engagement?

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **SQL** – Data extraction and business query analysis
- **Power BI** – Dashboard development and visualization
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
- **DAX** – KPI calculations and custom measures

# 📈 Key Business Insights

### 🍕 Classic Category Leads Sales

The Classic category generates the highest revenue and sales volume, indicating strong and consistent customer demand.

---

### 📅 Friday is the Peak Sales Day

Friday records the highest number of customer orders, making it an ideal day for promotional campaigns and combo offers.

---

### 📦 Large Pizzas are Most Preferred

Large-sized pizzas contribute the highest revenue among all pizza sizes, showing a clear customer preference.

---

### 🏆 Star Performers

Thai Chicken, BBQ Chicken, and Classic Deluxe consistently rank among the top-performing pizzas across revenue, orders, and quantity sold.

---

### 💰 Premium Revenue Drivers

California Chicken and Spicy Italian generate high revenue despite lower purchase frequency, suggesting they are higher-value menu items.

---

### 📈 Growth Opportunity

Hawaiian and Pepperoni have strong customer demand but comparatively lower revenue, creating opportunities for:

- Combo Offers
- Premium Add-ons
- Cross-selling
- Pricing Evaluation

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Promote top-performing pizzas through featured campaigns.
- Introduce Friday-exclusive combo offers.
- Bundle high-performing pizzas with lower-performing pizzas to increase product exposure.
- Upsell premium toppings and beverages to increase Average Order Value.
- Investigate factors contributing to the lower performance of bottom-selling pizzas.
